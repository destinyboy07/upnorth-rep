Upnorth Academy: Empowering Girls and Women in Technology Across Northern Nigeria.

Our mission is to provide girls and women in northern Nigeria with the skills, resources and support necessary to pursue careers in technology and create a brighter future. We believe that technology can empower individuals, transform communities, and change lives.

Our programs and initiatives

Coding Bootcamps

Intensive 12-week courses that teach you everything you need to know about web development, programming and software engineering.

Mentorship Programs

Connect with experienced professionals in the industry and get personalized guidance as you build your skills and advance your career.

Networking Events

Meet other tech enthusiasts, learn from industry leaders, and make valuable connections that could lead to your next professional opportunity.

Our Success Story

Senior dev

After completing Upnorth Academy's web development bootcamp, Senior dev landed a job at a fast-growing tech startup where he now leads a team of developers.

Favour

Thanks to Upnorth Academy's mentorship program, Favour was able to connect with a seasoned software engineer who not only helped her improve her coding skills but also assisted in landing her dream job.

Twin gees

Attending a networking session organized by Upnorth Academy was a turning point for the Twin gees. They were able to connect with entrepreneurs and tech leaders in their field, which gave them the motivation and confidence to launch their tech startup.
